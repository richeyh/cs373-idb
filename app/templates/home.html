What is The New York Times Bestsellers List?

From its first iteration in The New York Times on October 12, 1931, The New York Times Best Seller list has become the leading list monitored by book readers, writers, and publishers. The list is based on weekly sales reports of wholesalers, and independent and chain bookstores across the United States.
